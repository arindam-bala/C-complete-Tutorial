Properties of friend function
------------------------------
1. Not in the scope of class
2. Since it is not the scope of the class, it cannot 
    be called from the object of that class.
     
    c1.sumComplex() == Invalid;

3.Can be invoked without the help of any object
4.Usually contains the objects as arguments
5.Can be declared inside public or private section of 
  the class.

6. It cannot access the members directly by their 
   names and need object_name.member_name to access
   any member.
